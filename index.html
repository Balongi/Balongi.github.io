About Me

Game Developer!

As a Software Engineer at Markhor Games, I lead a team developing engaging multiplayer games for various platforms. I’ve overseen the creation and launch of 6+ multiplayer projects using technologies such as Fishnet Networking, Photon PUN 2, Photon Fusion, Photon Quantum, Firebase, and PlayFab. With experience at Markhor Games, BRIC Games, and The Game Storm Studios, I’ve developed over 40 simulation and FPS games. I’m skilled in PHP, C#, HTML5, CSS, and Unity, and I use GitHub to streamline team productivity and code management. I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science from The University of Lahore, and I’ve served as a Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador and beginner ethical hacker. I'm passionate about learning and eager to collaborate within the gaming industry.
